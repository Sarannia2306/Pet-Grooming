Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 7, 2025
SnugglePaw ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using our website and services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: name, email, phone, and password (hashed) when you register via Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile & Pet Information: pet name, type, breed, size, notes you add.
  • AI Processing Data: when you upload a pet image, we process it client‑side (in your browser) using TensorFlow.js to infer pet type/size/breed. Images are only uploaded to our storage when you save them as part of your profile.
  • Booking & Payment Data: selected service, date/time, amount, payment method. Payments are processed by PayPal; we receive payment confirmations/IDs but not your full card details.
  • Usage Data: device/browser info, IP address, and interactions necessary for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services (grooming/daycare/boarding booking, dashboard, reminders).
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts using Firebase Authentication.
  • Store appointments, invoices, and related records in Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Process payments via PayPal and record payment status.
  • Personalize user experience, including pre‑filling pet details using AI in your browser.
  • Communicate updates, confirmations, and service notices.

3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract: to provide booked services you request.
  • Legitimate Interests: to secure our platform and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: where required for marketing communications or optional cookies.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for auth/database/storage; PayPal for payments.
  • Legal & Safety: to comply with laws, enforce our Terms, or protect rights and safety.
  • We do not sell personal information.

5. Data Retention

We retain account, pet, and booking records as long as your account is active or as needed for our legitimate business and legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements).

6. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We may use essential cookies for session management and security, and limited analytics to understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. To exercise rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Security

We use industry‑standard measures and Firebase security features to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in regions where Firebase/PayPal infrastructure operates. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
