Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and more.

What services do you offer for pets?

We offer a comprehensive range of pet Paw services including professional grooming, dayPaw, boarding, and veterinary Paw. Our grooming services include baths, haircuts, nail trims, ear cleaning, and teeth brushing. Our dayPaw provides a safe and fun environment for your pets to socialize, and our boarding facilities offer comfortable accommodations for when you're away.

How do I book an appointment?

Booking an appointment is easy! You can book online through our website, give us a call, or visit our facility in person. For first-time clients, we recommend creating an account to easily manage your pet's appointments and access your pet's records. You can also download our mobile app for on-the-go booking and management.

What are your operating hours?

Our regular business hours are Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on major holidays. For boarding drop-off and pick-up, we have extended hours to accommodate travel schedules. Please check our website or call ahead for holiday hours and any special announcements.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your pet's safety is our top priority. We follow strict cleaning and sanitation protocols, require all pets to be up-to-date on vaccinations, and have trained staff members certified in pet first aid and CPR. Our facilities are equipped with security cameras, and we maintain a secure environment with controlled access. We also conduct temperament assessments for all pets to ensure compatibility in group settings.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans can change. For grooming appointments, we kindly request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. For boarding reservations, we require 48 hours' notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. During peak seasons, we may require a deposit to hold your reservation. Please refer to our complete cancellation policy for more details.
